How to make a fortune teller!

Step 1. Get a A4 size sheet of paper.

Step 2. Fold a corner, diagonally.

Step 3. Fold the corner too.

Step 4.Cut the excess.

Step 5.You now have a square paper.

Step 6. Fold 1/4 of the corner to the center as a reference point.

Step 7. Do the same with all four corners.

Step 8. Turn the paper over.

Step 9. Again do the same process. Fold it again.

Step 10. Fold them all to the center.

Step 11. Turn it over.

Step 12. All done!

Donee!!!
